## What is IndexOf.AI?

**IndexOf.AI** is an AI tool discovery and navigation platform. We curate, organize, and present AI tools across every major category — so users can find what they need fast, and makers can get their tools in front of the right audience.

- **For users** — Browse AI tools organized by category, compare features, and find the perfect fit for your workflow. No login required.
- **For developers & makers** — Submit your AI tool and reach an audience actively searching for solutions like yours. Free basic listing, or $2.99 one-time for a Pro listing with SEO boost.

> The AI landscape evolves fast. IndexOf.AI bridges the gap between AI builders and the people who need their tools.
